About Cyber Escape Room

Our escape room combines fun and education to teach cybersecurity concepts interactively. Students engage in real-world cybersecurity challenges, making learning engaging and meaningful.

Project Objectives

  • Learning Experience through Gaming: Incorporate fun and educational cybersecurity challenges like jeopardy, cryptography, code-breaking, and password creation.
  • Interactive Challenges: Develop a variety of puzzles and games, from ciphers and binary conversions to code-breaking and password security.
  • Cyber Awareness: Teach important topics like cyberbullying and digital safety.

How It Works

  1. Register: Sign up with your UTD email to gain access.
  2. Select a Challenge: Choose from various levels and topics.
  3. Solve & Learn: Complete puzzles to understand key cybersecurity concepts.
  4. Earn Rewards: Win points and badges for clearing levels.

Project Deliverables

  • Project Proposal: Comprehensive plan outlining project goals and implementation.
  • Fully Functional Website: Interactive website featuring the escape room challenges.
  • Meeting Minutes: Documentation of discussions, decisions, and progress.
  • Preliminary Design Report (PDR): Initial report covering design elements and strategies.
  • Final Design Report (FDR): Detailed final report highlighting results, outcomes, and evaluations.
